What is WonderBox?

WonderBox was created as a brand for our group’s second quarter IPBA in our Lifelabs subject.

With its current rise in trend and broad opportunities for creativity, we chose blind boxes as the product our brand offers, which we call as "UNBOXED."

To wonder is to be curious about something, and that is the also the essence of blind boxes, which comes from the excitement and anticipation of opening one.

The WonderBox logo combines a gift box and a question mark that is made into a face, to represent the wonder that people feel. It also includes our brand colors of Blue and Yellow.
