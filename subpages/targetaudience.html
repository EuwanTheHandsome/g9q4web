Target Audience



‎ Demographics:
‎ Age: 18–45 years old.
‎ Gender: Male and Female.
‎ Location: Primarily Filipino audiences (both local and international) and global philanthropic communities.
‎ Interests: Music, philanthropy, social justice, community development, helping people in need.
‎ Economic Status: Primarily middle-class and upper-class individuals with disposable income, and who are inclined to participate
‎ in charitable causes.

‎ Psychographics:
‎ • Passionate about music and the arts, particularly concerts with a charitable focus.
‎ • Interested in social causes, particularly issues related to poverty, education, and community development in rural areas.
‎ • Likely to engage with compelling storytelling and visuals that show needs.
‎ • Motivated by a desire to make a tangible difference and see real outcomes from their contributions.
‎ • Likely to participate in virtual and live events, and share cause-driven content on social media platforms. ‎
