Chosen Category



‎ Our chosen category is an “Educational Documentary”, with a focus on Concert Fundraising and the situation of the Sitio Bebe community.
‎
The channel will promote a fundraising concert for Sitio Bebe, using an educational documentary-style content to make a persuasive video that will make people give donations or join the concert. The focus will be on showcasing the Sitio Bebe community’s struggles, problems, and how the concert will help address their important needs. The educational aspect will not only highlight the event but also provide background information on the social and economic challenges that the residents of Sitio Bebe are experiencing.
